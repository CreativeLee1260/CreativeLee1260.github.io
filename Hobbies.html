Hobbies

One of my primary passions lies in creating a variety of artworks, including oil paintings, graphite sketches, and my favorite digital art. I am particularly interested to landscape oil painting, as the unpredictability of nature offers both a challenge and a sense of calmness. The role of light, color, and form in natural settings inspires me and enhances my creative process. I also do a lot of sketching as a way of studying objects, places, and figures. This practice not only sharpens my observational skills but also allows me to refine my fundamental art techniques such as shading and perspective. Through these diverse art mediums, I continually strive to deepen my understanding of art while capturing the beauty of the world around me. In the world of digital art, I explore the endless possibilities technology offers. By blending traditional techniques with modern creativity, I can create vibrant, immersive scenes that invite viewers to experience new worlds. Art overall makes me appericiate life and its beauty aswell as its worse. Art makes me observe the small things in life that the others simply ignores.
